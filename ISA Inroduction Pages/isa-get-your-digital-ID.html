Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for an ISA Digital ID?

All registered Igbo students at Federal University Wukari are eligible for the ISA Digital ID. You must be a paid member of the association to receive your ID card.

How long does it take to get my Digital ID?

Processing typically takes 3-5 working days after application submission. You'll receive your digital ID via email and it will be available in your ISA member portal.

Is there a fee for the Digital ID?

The initial Digital ID is free for all paid ISA members. Replacement cards may incur a small administrative fee of ₦500.

How do I use the QR code on my ID?

The QR code can be scanned at ISA events for quick check-in and verification. ISA officials will have scanners to validate your membership status.

What if I lose access to my Digital ID?

You can always access your ID through the ISA member portal. If you need assistance, contact the ISA secretariat for support in recovering your digital ID.
