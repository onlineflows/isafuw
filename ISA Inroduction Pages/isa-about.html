Who We Are

"Onye aghana nwanne ya" - Do not abandon your brother/sister

Founded in 2015, the Igbo Students Association (ISA) at Federal University Wukari serves as a cultural, academic, and social support system for Igbo students in Taraba State. We bridge the gap between our rich Igbo heritage and the diverse university community.

Our Mission

To preserve Igbo cultural identity while fostering academic excellence and leadership among our members through innovative programs and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading cultural student association that empowers Igbo students to thrive academically while maintaining strong connections to their heritage.

Our Journey

2015

Foundation

ISA FUW was established by 12 pioneer students to create a support system for Igbo students in Taraba State.

2017

First Cultural Day

Hosted our inaugural Igbo Cultural Day celebration, now an annual event attracting over 500 attendees.

2019

Academic Support Launch

Initiated our tutorial program, helping members achieve a 30% improvement in exam results.

2022

Alumni Network

Established formal connections with 50+ graduates now working in various professional fields.
