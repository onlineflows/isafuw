Contact FAQs

How quickly can I expect a response to my message?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during working days. For urgent matters, please call the executive phone numbers listed above.

Can I visit the ISA Secretariat anytime?

Our official office hours are Monday-Friday, 9AM-4PM. Weekend visits require prior appointment. During exam periods, hours may vary.

How can I join the ISA WhatsApp group?

After completing your membership registration, you'll receive an invitation link via email. Alternatively, message the PRO on WhatsApp (+234 814 567 8901) with your matric number.

Who should I contact for welfare assistance?

Our Welfare Officer handles all assistance requests. Call +234 815 678 9012 or email welfare@isafuw.org. For emergencies, contact the President directly.
