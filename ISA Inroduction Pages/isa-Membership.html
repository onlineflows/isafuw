Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join ISA FUW?

All Igbo students at Federal University Wukari are eligible for regular membership. Non-Igbo students with interest in Igbo culture can join as associate members.

How much are membership dues?

Annual membership fees are:
- Regular: ₦3,000
- Premium: ₦5,000
- Associate: ₦1,500

When does membership expire?

Membership is valid for one academic session (September - August). Renewal notices are sent via email and WhatsApp.

How do I get my membership card?

Digital membership cards are emailed within 48 hours of payment. Physical cards can be collected at the ISA Secretariat 2 weeks after registration.

Can I upgrade my membership?

Yes! You can upgrade anytime by paying the difference between your current membership and the premium tier.
