Igbo Language Revival

Our weekly "Asụsụ Igbo" classes help members learn and preserve our native language, with special focus on different dialects from across Igboland.

"Asụsụ bụ ndụ" - Language is life

Traditional Arts

From "Atilogwu" dance to "Uri" art workshops, we keep Igbo traditions alive through hands-on experiences and performances.
